Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions our clients ask most, so you can make the best decision for your business.

How quickly can I see results? +
Most clients begin seeing measurable improvements within the first 30 days of implementation. Our phased approach means you get quick wins early, like automated workflows and consolidated data, while the full solution is deployed over 3 to 6 months. We prioritize the changes that impact your bottom line fastest, so you see ROI sooner rather than later.
Will this work with my existing systems? +
Absolutely. Our solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with the tools you already use. Whether it's your accounting software, CRM, HR platform, or legacy systems, we build integrations that keep your data flowing without disruption. During our discovery phase, we map out every integration point so there are no surprises and no downtime for your team.
How much does it cost? +
Every business is different, so we provide custom quotes based on your specific needs and scope. We offer flexible pricing models, from one-time project fees to subscription-based plans, so you can choose what works best for your budget. The best way to get accurate pricing is to schedule a free consultation where we'll understand your needs and provide a transparent, no-surprise proposal.
Do you offer training and support? +
Yes! Through our dedicated training and upskilling arm, TSI (Techsavanna Training Institute), we provide comprehensive training programs for all our solutions. This includes on-site training, virtual instructor-led sessions, self-paced e-learning through ElimucoreLMS, and ongoing support. We ensure your team is fully equipped to maximize the value of your investment from day one, with refresher courses and advanced training available as your needs evolve.
How is TechSavanna different from other providers? +
Three things set us apart: First, we're built for African businesses. We understand the unique challenges of operating in this market. Second, we offer end-to-end solutions, from software to training to ongoing support, so you're never piecing together multiple vendors. Third, we're ISO 9001:2015 certified and accredited by the ICT Authority, which means you're partnering with a provider that meets the highest standards of quality and compliance.
Can I start small and scale later? +
Absolutely, and many of our most successful clients started exactly that way. Our solutions are modular by design, so you can begin with the features you need most and expand as your business grows. This approach means lower upfront investment, faster time-to-value, and the flexibility to adapt as your needs evolve. We'll help you plan a roadmap that makes sense for where you are today and where you want to be tomorrow. Get started with a free consultation →
